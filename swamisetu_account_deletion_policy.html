Account Deletion Policy

1. Introduction

This Account Deletion Policy outlines the process and conditions for deleting an account from the Swami Setu application. We value your privacy and aim to provide clear and transparent guidelines for account deletion.

2. Requesting Account Deletion

Users can request the deletion of their account at any time by following these steps:

  • Log In: Sign in to your account using your username and password.
  • Navigate to Account Settings: Go to the "Account Settings" section.
  • Find the Delete Account Option: Scroll down and find the "Delete Account" option.
  • Confirm Deletion: Click on "Delete Account" and follow the prompts to confirm your decision. You may be asked to provide your password again for security purposes.

3. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Account Deletion Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Account Deletion Policy or need assistance with the account deletion process, please contact our support team at swamisetu1313@gmail.com.
